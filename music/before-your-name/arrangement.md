# Before Your Name - Arrangement

## Core Sound

- Tempo: 100 BPM
- Meter: 4/4
- Key center: C major, with suspended and added-second colors
- Texture: Solo father guitar or voice with room for unanswered rests
- Backing rule: no generated guitar backing. Guitar is reserved for live/user performance.

## Parts

### Voice/Guitar 1 - Father

- Begins alone with a confident but restrained picked pattern.
- Uses open-position shapes and warm voicings.
- Leaves a full measure of silence after the first phrase, as if expecting a response.
- Becomes less ornamental by the final chorus.

### Voice/Guitar 2 - Son

- Not present as an active voice yet.
- Represented by silence and one distant imagined answer near the end of the MIDI sketch.

### Harmony Bed

- Suggested progression: Cadd2, G/B, Am7, Fmaj7, C/E, Gsus, C.
- Keep accompaniment sparse. The father's uncertainty should be audible.

### No-Guitar Backing Parts

- Length: matched to the generated vocal-only stem form: intro, verse 1, verse 2, chorus, bridge, final chorus, and tag.
- Bass: warm root motion, entering gently below the vocal.
- Drums: soft pulse only, no full kit energy; support the 100 BPM phrasing.
- Piano: sparse chord bed using Cadd2/G/B/Am7/Fmaj7 colors.
- Pad: warm air underneath sustained moments.
- Celeste: optional nursery-light color, used sparingly.
